Supply chain delays impact Apple's Q3 production targets amid growing market pressure

Apple's supply chain disruptions have intensified in recent weeks, with multiple suppliers reporting significant delays expected to impact Q3 production targets by as much as 15%. Industry analysts have begun revising estimates downward as the company faces increasing pressure from both logistics partners and component manufacturers.

The situation has been compounded by geopolitical tensions affecting key manufacturing hubs in Southeast Asia. Three of Apple's primary contract manufacturers have reported capacity constraints, leading to a cascading effect across the product pipeline.

Despite these challenges, Apple has outlined contingency measures including diversified supplier relationships and strategic inventory buffers built up over the previous two quarters.
